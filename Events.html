• No candidate should be an author of more than one paper submitted.
• Team members need not belong to the same institution.
• No limit on the number of participations from an institution.

TOPICS
• Topics for Paper Presentation 1 (Thermal)
> Computational Heat transfer
> Combustion Engines
> FEM/FEA/CFD
> Energy systems
> Alternate fuels
• Topics for Paper Presentation 2 (Manufacturing)
> Modern Manufacturing
> Cutting tool design
> Composite materials
> Metal forming techniques
> Rapid prototyping
> NVH

PAPER SUBMISSION
• Working Models,Samples Fetch You Extra Points

• Bring your Soft Copies in CD or Pen drive.
• Paper should not exceed eight pages.
• Details of the team members: Name, Department, E-Mail ID, Contact number, Institution name Should be sent along with the Paper.
• Deadline for submission: On or before 3.03.2012

PRESENTATION DETAILS:

RC Cars


For the car:
• The dimensions of the car should not exceed 25 * 20 cms.
• The car should be powered by electric motor only.
• More than one electric motor can be used in the car.
• The chassis of the car has to be designed by the participant.
• Ready-made gearbox, remote control can be taken from toy cars.
• Cars should be controlled with Wire/Wireless controllers.
• On board power source should be used.
• Maximum of 12V can be given to the car excluding power supply to the remote.
• No external power supply is to be used.
• Qualifying round may be conducted if necessary.

For the teams:
• Each team should consist of two participants.
• Teams should assemble earlier, for inspection of the cars.
• Cars not meeting the specifications listed above will lead to disqualification.
• Teams not reaching on time will be disqualified.
• Any act against the spirit of the game will also lead to disqualification.
